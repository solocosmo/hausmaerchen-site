Solocosmo Studios is a small independent studio that makes carefully-finished iOS apps.

Every tale is edited by hand. The collections are finite — you buy the book and the book is yours. There are no subscriptions and no servers, because there is nothing to host: each app is a self-contained library that runs entirely on your device.
