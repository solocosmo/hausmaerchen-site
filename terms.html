What Hausmärchen is

Hausmärchen is a native iOS reading app published by Solocosmo Studios. It bundles two hundred and forty-five tales of the Brothers Grimm — the canonical 1857 corpus, the 1812/1815 paralipomena (Lost Tales), and the ten Children's Legends — in four reading variants, with illustrations from seventeen public-domain and contemporary artists. The corpus is finite and self-contained. The app does not stream, fetch, or update content over the network.

The purchase is processed entirely by Apple. We do not see your payment details.

No account, no server

There is no Hausmärchen account. There is no Hausmärchen server. Your favorites, last-read position, parent PIN hash, and unlock entitlement live on the device. We have nothing to lose, breach, sell, or hand over because we hold nothing.

Children's Mode and parental responsibility

Children's Mode is a feature provided in good faith for parents and guardians who want to share the gentler tales with a child unattended. It is parent-locked behind a four-digit PIN with an escalating cooldown after three wrong tries. We make no guarantee that any technical filter is perfect; you remain the responsible adult. The Junior rewrites have been written specifically to remove on-page violence, but the tales themselves are still old tales — sorrow, danger, and magic do not vanish from them.
